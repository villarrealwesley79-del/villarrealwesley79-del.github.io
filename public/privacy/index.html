We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

Personal Data:

Automatically Collected Information:

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses cookies to analyze how you use this website. The information generated about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google uses this information for the purposes of:

  • Evaluating your use of the website
  • Compiling reports on website activity for website operators

Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies and Similar Technologies#

This website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet

Cookie Types#

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
