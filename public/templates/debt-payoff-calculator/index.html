Frequently Asked Questions#

Q: What’s the difference between snowball and avalanche? A: Snowball pays smallest debts first for quick wins. Avalanche pays highest interest first to minimize total interest. This template shows both so you can choose.

Q: How many debts can I track? A: The template supports up to 15 different debts with individual balances, interest rates, and minimum payments.

Q: Can I see how extra payments help? A: Yes. Enter different extra payment amounts and instantly see how your payoff date and total interest change.

Q: Does it work for student loans? A: Yes. The calculator works for any type of debt: credit cards, student loans, car loans, personal loans, medical debt, or mortgages.
