Debt Payoff Calculator: Snowball vs Avalanche Method Explained

The debt snowball and debt avalanche are the two most popular methods for paying off multiple debts. Both work. Both get you debt-free. They differ in strategy, psychology, and how much interest you pay. Understanding the math behind each helps you choose the right approach for your situation. The Debt Snowball Method The snowball method targets debts from smallest to largest balance, regardless of interest rate. How it works: List all debts in order of balance (smallest first) Make minimum payments on all debts Put extra money toward the smallest debt Once the smallest is paid, roll that payment amount to the next smallest Repeat until all debts are gone The psychological appeal is obvious.
